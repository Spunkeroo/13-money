How to Track Crypto Prices for Free in 2026: Best Tools and Dashboards

March 6, 2026 · 8 min read · 13.Money

You don't need a $50/month Bloomberg terminal to track cryptocurrency prices. Between free APIs, browser bookmarks, and aggregator sites, you can build a real-time crypto monitoring setup in under an hour at zero cost. Here's exactly what to use and how.

The Top Free Crypto Price Trackers

1. CoinGecko (Best Overall)

CoinGecko tracks 13,000+ cryptocurrencies with real-time prices, market caps, volume, and historical data. It's free, comprehensive, and includes:

  • Portfolio tracker (create an account, add your holdings)
  • Price alerts via email
  • API access — 30 calls/minute free, enough for personal dashboards
  • Derivatives, DeFi, and NFT data

CoinGecko Pro adds more API calls and advanced features, but the free tier is sufficient for most users.

2. CoinMarketCap (Best for Volume Data)

CoinMarketCap is the most recognized crypto price site. Free features include:

  • Real-time prices for 24,000+ assets
  • Exchange rankings by volume
  • Fear & Greed Index (useful sentiment indicator)
  • Price alerts (requires free account)
  • Gainers and losers lists (great for spotting momentum)

3. TradingView (Best for Charts)

TradingView's free tier provides powerful charting for all major crypto pairs. Features include:

  • 50+ technical indicators
  • Drawing tools (trend lines, Fibonacci, channels)
  • Multi-chart layouts
  • Price alerts via email and app
  • Social feed where traders share ideas

The free plan limits you to 3 active alerts and 1 chart layout — enough for casual tracking.

4. Messari (Best for Research)

Messari goes beyond price data to offer crypto project research, sector analysis, and protocol metrics. The free tier includes solid fundamental data most retail investors don't bother reading — which is your edge.

5. DefiLlama (Best for DeFi)

If you hold DeFi tokens, DefiLlama tracks TVL (total value locked), protocol revenues, and chain activity across every major DeFi platform. Completely free, no account needed.

6. 13.Money Live Dashboard

Our free real-time dashboard aggregates Bitcoin, Ethereum, and top crypto prices alongside gold and stock indices — all updating live, no signup required.

Setting Up Price Alerts for Free

Price alerts prevent you from having to constantly watch charts. Here's where to set them for free:

  • CoinGecko: Email alerts for any asset crossing a price threshold
  • TradingView: Up to 3 alerts on the free plan (email + app notification)
  • Coinbase app: Price alerts even without trading
  • Telegram bots: Numerous free bots (CryptoSignals, Cornix alerts) can monitor prices and ping you instantly

Using Free APIs to Build Your Own Dashboard

If you're comfortable with basic JavaScript or Python, free APIs let you build a custom dashboard:

  • CoinGecko API: Free, 30 calls/min, comprehensive data
  • Binance Public API: Real-time OHLCV data for all trading pairs, no authentication required
  • Kraken WebSocket API: Live streaming price feeds, free
Pro tip: The single most useful free crypto tool most people ignore is the Bitcoin mempool visualizer at mempool.space — it shows network congestion, fee rates, and transaction speeds in real time. Essential if you're moving BTC.
